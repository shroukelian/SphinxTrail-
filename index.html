Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the tours?

All tours include transportation, accommodation, and guided services unless specified otherwise in the tour package details.

How do I book?

You can book online through our website's booking form, or contact our 24/7 support team via WhatsApp for direct assistance.

Are the tours family-friendly?

Yes! Our tours are designed to be suitable for families with children of all ages, ensuring safety and entertainment for everyone.

Do I need travel insurance?

We strongly recommend travel insurance for all international trips to cover unexpected events, although it is not mandatory for booking.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel up to 7 days before the tour start date for a full refund. Specific conditions may apply to luxury Nile cruises.

Are meals included?

Some tours include full board or half board meals; please check the specific tour details for what is included in your chosen package.

Can I customize my tour?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable private tours tailored to your specific interests, budget, and time frame.

What languages are the tours available in?

Our tours are primarily available in English and Arabic. We can provide guides for Spanish, French, German, and Italian on request.
